HOW TO PLAY

⚔️ Attacking

Perform advanced dribbles using the right stick. Smart Assist helps you move in the right direction automatically and avoid defenders who draw close.

🛡️ Defending

Use tactical pressing and intelligent positioning to win the ball back. Time your tackles perfectly to regain possession without conceding fouls.

🎯 Shooting

Master power shots and finesse curls. The shooting system rewards timing and positioning — find the right angle and beat the keeper.

📋 Team Building

Combine current stars with football legends in Dream Team mode. Chemistry and formation are the keys to building a winning squad.
